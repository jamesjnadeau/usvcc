Corporate Members

Overview

As a USVCC Corporate Member, you’ll have a unique opportunity to partner with a cutting edge organization benefiting transitioning military, veterans, and their families. Your company will help provide services, monetary support, and positively impact and shape the future of our country’s servicemen and women.

Opportunities, services, and Veteran Owned Business access is outlined in Membership Benefits &Dues.

Membership Benefits &Dues

100,000+ veteran businesses

Unlimited national access to veteran businesses through a searchable database.

Showcase your veteran programs

Integrate your corporate veteran programs, services, and initiatives into the USVCC website, and menu of services, made accessible to the USVCC’s veteran owned businesses, all veterans, and transitioning military.

Free stringent live certification program

Access to USVCC’s nationally-recognized, stringent certification program for your non-certified veteran owned business to assist you in identifying legitimate USVCC Certified Businesses within your supply chain.

Custom Supplier Development &Capacity Building for Corporate Supply Chain

Participation in the USVCC’s supplier diversity series. This educational and idea-sharing series addresses the critical issues veteran business professionals face on a daily basis in a solutions-based format.

USVCC Five Pillar Committee Member

Become a committee member to one of the USVCC’s Five Pillars: Employment, Education, Business, Wellness, and Family

Government and Commercial Diversity Validation and Reporting

Under federal acquisition regulations (FAR), state, and municipal regulation compliance.

National recognition

Corporate recognition on USVCC website, social media, press releases, and events (i.e. Bi-Annual Senator Blumenthal Veteran Roundtable), and national events benefiting transitioning military.
