Overview

United States Veterans Chamber of Commerce membership is open to all Veteran Owned Businesses (VOB), Service Disabled Veteran Owned Businesses (SDVOB), Veterans, Gold Star Businesses, Veteran Spouse Businesses, and 100% Disabled Veteran Caregiver businesses. 

USVCC members are placed within a network of state chambers across the United States allowing for a local support system while having access to local and national resources, programs, and services under the USVCC’s five pillars of support: Education, Employment, Business, Wellness, and Family.
