About USVCC

The USVCC serves all transitioning military, veterans, veteran owned businesses, gold star spouses, spouses of 100% disabled veterans, and their families.

About the USVCC

United States Veterans Chamber of Commerce (USVCC) is an IRS designated, nonprofit 501(c)3 organization. We connect corporate America, government, and small businesses to our nation’s veterans and the USVCC’s 100,000+ veteran owned businesses.

USVCC supports all transitioning military, veterans, veteran owned businesses, and their families through the USVCC’s holistic Five Pillar approach: Employment, Education, Wellness, Business, and Family.

USVCC 's Mission

Our mission is to support all transitioning military, veterans, veteran owned businesses, and their families through the USVCC’s holistic Five Pillar approach: Employment, Education, Wellness, Business, and Family.

Through a relationship with the U.S. Department of Defense, USVCC applies the Five Pillar support model to more than 20,000 high- risk, medically tagged service members annually to aid in the transition to veteran status.
