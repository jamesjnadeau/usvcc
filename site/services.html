USVCC Services

DoD Warrior Games

Since inception in 2010, the Department of Defense (DoD) Warrior Games has provided an opportunity for wounded, ill, and injured service members and veterans. Representing teams from the U.S. Army, Marine Corps, Navy, Air Force, Coast Guard and U.S. Special Operations Command (SOCOM) to work through their limitations, express their inner selves, and recapture their sense of wholeness and belonging.

Employment

Gainful employment is the bedrock for transitioning military, veterans, and their families assimilating back into civilian life paving the way to bright futures and healthy families.

  • State-of-the-art employment portal
  • Workforce development service
  • Corporate culture classes
  • Resume building with military to corporate skills translator
  • Sustainability
  • Employment mentorship
  • Transitioning services
  • Interview skills

Education

Education is a core component to an improved quality of life for transitioning military, veterans, and their families. With many programs starting before service members leave military service, USVCC assists in skill development needed today and for the future.

  • Veteran friendly University and College catalogue
  • Entrepreneurship Program
  • Scholarships
  • Life skills
  • Industry and Technology Certifications

Wellness

Wellness is paramount to sustained success within the other USVCC pillars: Employment, Education, Business, and Family.

  • Access to physical and medical rehabilitative services
  • Respite and care giving services
  • Mental health services
  • Spiritual health services

Business

Due to military training, tenacity, and perseverance, veteran owned businesses are uniquely suited to succeed in business. USVCC provides tools and services focused on giving veteran owned businesses a hand up and drive business connectivity to corporate America, government entities, and small businesses.

  • Veteran owned business (VOB) certification
  • VOB Supplier Development
  • VOB Capacity Building for Corporate Supply Chain
  • Corporate and Government Matchmaking
  • Access to financial tools and services
  • Leadership Scholarships
  • Mentoring
  • Business Continuity Services
  • Capability Statements

Family

Family is the core foundation of all transitioning military and veterans seeking a successful return to a fulfilling and meaningful life.

  • Military and veteran family services
  • Residential and housing services
  • Respite and supportive services
  • Caregiving Services

Strategic Relationships
